Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Photoshop?
Photoshop requires manual compositing—matching lighting, adding shadows, adjusting perspective by hand. Ature's AI handles all of that automatically. What takes 30 minutes in Photoshop takes 30 seconds here.
Can I upload my own furniture photos?
Yes! Upload any furniture image—product photos, catalog cuts, or photos you've taken. The AI will isolate the furniture and blend it naturally into your room scenes.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. Your projects, assets, and generated images are completely private to your account. We don't train AI on your data, share it with third parties, or expose it to other users.
Do I need technical skills?
Not at all. If you can drag-and-drop files and type a sentence, you can use Ature. The AI handles all the technical complexity of compositing and rendering.
